# Content Creation Runbooks

Frameworks and templates for creating and promoting content.

## Available Runbooks

| Runbook | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| [hyperlink-research.md](hyperlink-research.md) | Find authoritative links for people and organizations |
| [social-media-post.md](social-media-post.md) | Template for social media posts about events |
| [blog-promotion.md](blog-promotion.md) | Template for promoting blog posts on social media |
| [content-promotion-framework.md](content-promotion-framework.md) | Comprehensive framework for strategic content promotion |

## Customization

The content-promotion-framework requires a voice profile to produce ready-to-use output. Add your voice profile in your personal ai-knowledge repo with:
- Your professional positioning
- Your authentic voice characteristics
- Words and patterns to avoid
- Your strategic approach to demonstrating expertise
