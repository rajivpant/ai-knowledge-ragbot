# Dataset Templates

Starter templates for personal datasets. Copy and fill in with your own information.

## Available Templates

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [about-me.md](about-me.md) | Personal background, interests, goals, values |
| [professional.md](professional.md) | Work history, skills, education, projects |
| [preferences.md](preferences.md) | Communication style, learning preferences, pet peeves |

## Usage

1. Copy these templates to your personal ai-knowledge repo
2. Replace all placeholder text with your actual information
3. Add or remove sections based on what's relevant to you

The more specific information you provide, the more personalized your AI assistant responses will be.

## Privacy Note

Only include information you're comfortable with your AI assistant knowing. These files stay local on your machine but are sent to AI APIs during conversations.
