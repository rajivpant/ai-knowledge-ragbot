# Instruction Templates

Starter templates for custom instructions. Copy and customize for your own use.

## Available Templates

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [default.md](default.md) | General-purpose AI assistant configuration |
| [creative-writer.md](creative-writer.md) | Creative writing coach and editor |
| [technical-advisor.md](technical-advisor.md) | Senior technical advisor for software engineering |

## Usage

1. Copy the template to your personal ai-knowledge repo
2. Customize the placeholders with your specific information
3. Add your own voice profile and preferences

These templates provide structure. Your customizations make them effective.
